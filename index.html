Biography

My interests primarily focus on machine learning systems. Before that, I interned at Databricks, PingCAP, Microsoft, ByteDance, and Kuaishou, working on cache, cloud database systems, ML infra, and graph database systems.

I was fortunate to be advised by Prof. Zhichao Cao, working on LSM-Tree based key-value store. Also, I worked as a research intern at Cornell Computer Systems Laboratory, advised by Prof. Udit Gupta.
